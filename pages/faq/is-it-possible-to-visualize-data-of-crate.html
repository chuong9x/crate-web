title: Is it possible to visualize data of Crate? link: https://crate.io/faq/is-it-possible-to-visualize-data-of-crate.html author: Nils Magnus description: Answer about frequently asked question about visualization. created: 2016-05-17 post_name: is-it-possible-to-visualize-data-of-crate status: publish post_type: faq weight: 1 Yes, we have many clients doing this. Crate works well with Metabase, Tableau, Stream Sets, Kibana, Zoom Data, and more. Other tools may connect via JDBC and ODBC (with support contract) client drivers. You can find [a comprehensive list here](/docs/tools/#data-visualization).
