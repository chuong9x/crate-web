title: Why does Crate claim to be faster in aggregations than Elasticsearch? link: https://crate.io/faq/why-does-crate-claim-to-be-faster-in-aggregations-than-elasticsearch.html author: Nils Magnus description: Answer about frequently asked question about elasticsearch. created: 2016-05-17 post_name: why-does-crate-claim-to-be-faster-in-aggregations-than-elasticsearch status: publish post_type: faq weight: 1 Crate uses each cluster node to perform aggregations in a distributed way. This means that the initial node just needs to combine the pre-aggregated results, leading to speed improvements.
