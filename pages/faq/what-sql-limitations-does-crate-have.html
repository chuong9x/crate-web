title: What SQL limitations does Crate have? link: https://crate.io/faq/what-sql-limitations-does-crate-have.html author: Nils Magnus description: Answer about frequently asked question about sql, limits. created: 2016-05-17 post_name: what-sql-limitations-does-crate-have status: publish post_type: faq weight: 1 Crate can't handle transactions, but implements eventual consistency. Not all forms of JOINs are supported due to the potential performance overhead. [Cross and inner joins are supported](/a/sql-for-elasticsearch), and we are adding others in the future. These features will affect INSERT and query performance.
