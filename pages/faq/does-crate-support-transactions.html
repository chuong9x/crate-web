title: Does Crate support transactions? link: https://crate.io/faq/does-crate-support-transactions.html author: Nils Magnus description: Does Crate support transactions? created: 2016-05-17 post_name: does-crate-support-transactions status: publish post_type: faq weight: 1 Crate does not support transactions as it focusses on eventual consistency for high performance. Crate can achieve '[optimistic concurrency control](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optimistic_concurrency_control)' through use of the `_version` column, [read more here](/docs/reference/sql/occ.html).
