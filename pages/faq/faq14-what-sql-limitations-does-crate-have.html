title: What SQL limitations does Crate have? link: https://crate.io/faq/faq14-what-sql-limitations-does-crate-have.html author: Nils Magnus description: Answer about frequently asked question about sql, limits. created: 2016-05-17 post_name: faq14-what-sql-limitations-does-crate-have status: publish post_type: faq weight: 1 Crate can't do transactions, but implements eventual consistency. Not all forms of JOINs are supported as part of the database as this is expensive. [Cross and inner joins are supported](https://crate.io/a/sql-for-elasticsearch), though. Having these features is intrinsically expensive in terms of execution of INSERT and query performance. However, we are adding more and more features as time goes by.
