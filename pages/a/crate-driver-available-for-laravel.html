title: Crate Driver Available for Laravel link: https://crate.io/blog/crate-driver-available-for-laravel/ author: Chris Ward description: The popular PHP Framework Laravel now has a Crate driver available. created: 2015-06-19 post_name: crate-driver-available-for-laravel status: publish post_type: post tags: php, crate, driver category: developernews One of the best things about being an open source community is the unexpected contributions that appear. One of our development team was following an issue for our [PHP PDO driver](https://github.com/crate/crate-pdo) when he noticed that a participant in the issue mentioned they had created a driver for [Laravel](http://laravel.com), a popular PHP framework. The [Driver](https://github.com/RatkoR/laravel-crate.io) supports all latest versions of Laravel and supports *eloquent*, Laravel's ORM system. The driver supports creating and dropping scheme, full text search, querying, tests and much more.
