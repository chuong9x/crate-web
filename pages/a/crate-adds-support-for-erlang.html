title: Crate Adds Support For Erlang link: https://crate.io/blog/crate-adds-support-for-erlang/ author: Spanky description: Craterl brings full SQL support including parameters and bulk operations, full BLOB support, for GET, PUT, DELETE and HEAD requests. created: 2014-12-04 post_name: crate-adds-support-for-erlang status: publish post_type: post tags: erlang, crate, driver category: developernews CrateIO is really happy to announce that we've released the first version of our [Erlang driver](https://github.com/crate/craterl) for Crate! It's important for us here at Crate to [support our developers](/community/), and with the release of "Craterl", now we can support a wider developer base. Craterl brings full SQL support including parameters and bulk operations, full BLOB support, for `GET`, `PUT`, `DELETE` and `HEAD` requests.
