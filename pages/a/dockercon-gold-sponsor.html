After attending DockerCon in Amsterdam last year we fell in love with the Docker community and have been involved ever since. Docker Swarm and Docker Compose made their debut in at DockerCon last year (we have found both tools to be extremely helpful) and we can't wait to see what announcements will be made this year. Join the Crate.IO team at the Hackathon, come to one of our talks during the conference, or visit us at our booth. Get your DockerCon tickets today!
